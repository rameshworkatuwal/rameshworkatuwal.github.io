June 2026

Moving from Kathmandu to Dubai is not a small decision. It's been months of planning, research, and preparation. But I'm ready — and I want to share why.

The Decision

With 7+ years of experience in digital marketing, content creation, and accounting, I felt I had built a strong foundation in Nepal. But to grow further — both professionally and financially — Dubai made complete sense. The UAE is one of the most dynamic job markets in the world, with massive demand for digital skills.

The Preparation

I've spent the last several months upgrading my skills — completing LinkedIn Learning certifications in logistics, Excel, bookkeeping, and communication. I've also built this portfolio website to make it easier for Dubai recruiters to find and evaluate my work.

What I'm Looking Forward To

The energy, the opportunity, and the diversity of Dubai excites me. I'm open to full-time and contract roles in digital marketing, content creation, social media management, and related fields. If you're a recruiter reading this — let's connect!

How I Produce 10+ Videos Per Day
May 2026

When I tell people I produce 10+ videos per day, they always ask: how? The answer is systems, tools, and practice.

My Tools

I use CapCut for fast edits — it's incredibly efficient for short-form content. For more polished work, I switch to Adobe Premiere Pro. Canva handles thumbnails and visual assets quickly.

The Workflow

Batching is the key. I script multiple videos at once, then record or edit in focused sessions. Templates save enormous time — once a style is established, replicating it is fast.

Platform Optimization

TikTok, Facebook Reels, and YouTube Shorts each have their nuances. I optimize captions, thumbnails, and length for each platform separately — what works on TikTok doesn't always work on YouTube.

The biggest lesson: consistency beats perfection. Showing up every day with good content always outperforms occasional "perfect" content.

SEO Tips That Actually Work in 2026
April 2026

After years of writing and editing SEO content, I've seen trends come and go. Here's what's actually working right now.

Search Intent First

Google has gotten extremely good at understanding intent. Keyword stuffing is dead. What matters now is whether your content genuinely answers what the user is looking for — completely and clearly.

E-E-A-T Still Matters

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness — Google rewards content from people who clearly know what they're talking about. First-person experience, real examples, and credentials help enormously.

Content Depth Over Length

Longer isn't always better. A focused 800-word article that fully answers a question beats a padded 3000-word article every time. Cover the topic completely, then stop.

Internal Linking

One of the most underrated SEO tactics. Linking related articles together keeps users on your site longer and signals topical authority to Google. Build topic clusters, not isolated pages.
